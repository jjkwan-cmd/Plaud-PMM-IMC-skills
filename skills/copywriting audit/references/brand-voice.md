## The four voice principles

| Voice Pillar | Expressed As | In Practice |
|---|---|---|
| **Intelligent** | Clarity with Depth | Use precision, simplicity, and insight. Make complexity easy, not shallow. |
| **Professional** | Confidence without Noise | Calm authority and grounded reasoning. No hype. |
| **Human** | Human-Centered Intelligence | Write for collaboration, not competition. Respect expertise. |
| **Secure** | Trusted Integrity | Reinforce trust, transparency, and responsibility in every message. |

## Preferred language

**Do use — verbs:** capture, extract, utilize, amplify, align, transform, act, empower, bridge  
**Do use — nouns:** intelligence, insight, context, decision, action, companion, presence, structure  
**Do use — adjectives (sparingly):** intelligent, seamless, reliable, trusted, refined  

**Never use:** amazing, revolutionary, cutting-edge, next-level, game-changing, abstract metaphors, vague superlatives
