# Plaud Brand Tone Library

# **How to Use This Library**

This library defines how Plaud's voice adapts across content types, not by changing who we are, but by adjusting how we sound in each context.

Each section gives you:

→  Tone dimensions calibrated for that content type

→  Structural rules specific to that format

→  Do / Don't examples drawn from real Plaud copy principles

Voice = constant. Tone = adaptive. These are the dials. 

This Library is brand tone variants, for full brand voice and tone, please see [Plaud Brand Voice & Tone Guide](https://www.notion.so/Plaud-Brand-Voice-Tone-Guide-2fcd0604616080d9aa67c0cde2621286?pvs=21)

## **01 Ad Copy**

Performance-driven copy that earns attention fast. Every word works. Angle leads, benefit lands, action follows.

**TONE DIMENSIONS**

| **Dimension** | **Setting** | **What this means** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Formality** | **Low–Medium** | Conversational but precise. No slang, no filler. |
| **Emotion** | **Focused** | Confident, not urgent. Inspire action without pressure. |
| **Energy** | **Crisp & decisive** | Short rhythm. Every sentence earns its place. |
| **Confidence** | **Strong & assured** | Lead with the outcome. Let the product speak for itself. |

**STRUCTURAL RULES**

→  Formula: Action + Performance + Benefit (e.g. "Capture every meeting, automatically — so you can stay fully present.")

→  Short-form (≤10 words): one idea, one verb, one clear outcome.

→  Long-form (≤30 words): angle A + proof point + CTA. No more than two sentences.

→  Headline: sentence case. CTA: Title Case, verb-first (e.g. "Shop NotePin S").

→  No exclamation marks. No em dashes. No rhetorical questions.

→  Connect to at least one pillar: Capture, Extract, or Utilize.

**DO / DON'T — EXAMPLES**

| **✓  DO** | **✕  DON'T** |
| --- | --- |
| *Never miss what matters. Plaud NotePin S captures every word, hands-free.* | *Revolutionary AI technology that will change how you take notes forever!* |
| *Capture meetings. Extract insights. Plaud does the work.* | *Cutting-edge AI-powered note-taking solution for modern professionals.* |
| *Your meetings, transcribed. Your decisions, structured. Your time, protected.* | *Amazing new features that make your meetings more productive and seamless.* |
| *SOC 2 certified. HIPAA compliant. Your conversations stay yours.* | *Ultra-secure, next-level privacy that keeps your data 100% safe at all times!* |
| *Ten grams. Unlimited clarity. Wear it. Capture everything.* | *Lightweight and innovative — the game-changer in AI note-taking technology.* |

## **02 EDM (Email)**

Direct, purposeful, and structured. Email earns a reader's time, honor it with brevity, clarity, and one clear ask.

**TONE DIMENSIONS**

| **Dimension** | **Setting** | **What this means** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Formality** | **Medium** | Professional but warm. Avoid stiff formality. |
| **Emotion** | **Calm & purposeful** | No hype, no urgency bait. Reader decides at their own pace. |
| **Energy** | **Measured** | Clear structure. Short paragraphs. One idea per section. |
| **Confidence** | **Guiding** | Direct recommendations without pressure. Helpful, not salesy. |

**STRUCTURAL RULES**

→  Subject line: ≤50 characters. Outcome-led. Sentence case. No punctuation at end.

→  Preview text: extends the subject — not a repeat of it.

→  Body: one primary message. Max two supporting points. Single CTA per email.

→  Paragraphs: 2–3 sentences max. Short line length. White space is intentional.

→  CTA button: Title Case, verb-first, 2–4 words (e.g. "Explore NotePin S", "Read the Report").

→  Salutation: "Hi [Name]" — not "Dear", not "Hey".

→  Sign-off: simple. "The Plaud team" or the sender's name.

**DO / DON'T — EXAMPLES**

| **✓  DO** | **✕  DON'T** |
| --- | --- |
| *Subject: Your meeting insights, structured automatically* | *Subject: HUGE NEWS: Check out what's new at Plaud!!! 🎉🎉* |
| *Hi Sarah, Plaud NotePin S now integrates with Notion, your action items land there automatically.* | *Dear Valued Customer, We are thrilled to announce an amazing new revolutionary feature!* |
| *One clip. Every conversation captured. Nothing to set up.* | *Our cutting-edge, next-level AI technology is now even more powerful and innovative.* |
| *CTA: Try It Free* | *CTA: CLICK HERE NOW TO GET STARTED TODAY!!!* |
| *Plaud captures the meeting. You stay present. Everything else is handled.* | *Leveraging the power of AI, our solution seamlessly transforms your workflow going forward.* |

## **03 PR Boilerplate**

The authoritative, permanent record of who Plaud is. Factual, composed, and written for journalists, partners, and institutional audiences.

**TONE DIMENSIONS**

| **Dimension** | **Setting** | **What this means** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Formality** | **High** | Structured, institutional. Press-ready from first read. |
| **Emotion** | **Calm & composed** | No excitement markers. Authority comes from facts, not adjectives. |
| **Energy** | **Measured & deliberate** | Unhurried. Every sentence adds a fact or credential. |
| **Confidence** | **Quietly assured** | The summary speaks for itself. No superlatives needed. |

**STRUCTURAL RULES**

→  Open with the positioning statement: "Plaud is building the world's most trusted AI work companion for professionals."

→  Include: user count (1,500,000+), founding year (2023), incorporation (Delaware), HQ (San Francisco).

→  List all active certifications exactly: ISO 27001, ISO 27701, SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, EN18031.

→  Mission statement verbatim: "Amplify human intelligence."

→  Vision statement verbatim: "...capture, extract, and utilize intelligence from what you say, hear, see, and think."

→  Two paragraphs maximum. Do not add marketing language or campaign copy.

→  Never use: "leading," "best-in-class," "innovative," "revolutionary," or any unverifiable claim.

**DO / DON'T — EXAMPLES**

| **✓  DO** | **✕  DON'T** |
| --- | --- |
| *Plaud is building the world's most trusted AI work companion for professionals to elevate productivity and performance through note-taking solutions, trusted by over 1,500,000 users worldwide since 2023.* | *Plaud is a leading, innovative AI company revolutionizing how professionals capture information with their amazing, cutting-edge products.* |
| *With full ISO 27001, ISO 27701, SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, and EN18031 compliance, Plaud is committed to the highest standards of data security and privacy protection.* | *Plaud takes security very seriously and has lots of certifications to make sure your data is safe and secure.* |
| *With a mission to amplify human intelligence, Plaud is building the next-generation intelligence infrastructure and interfaces to capture, extract, and utilize what you say, hear, see, and think.* | *Plaud's mission is to help people be more productive using powerful AI tools that leverage cutting-edge technology to transform workflows.* |
| *Plaud Inc. is a Delaware-incorporated, San Francisco-based company pushing the boundary of human–AI intelligence through a hardware–software combination.* | *Plaud is a startup disrupting the note-taking space with a game-changing combination of hardware and software.* |

## **04 Landing Page**

The full expression of a product or campaign. Structured for skimmers and readers alike — each section earns the scroll.

**TONE DIMENSIONS**

| **Dimension** | **Setting** | **What this means** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Formality** | **Medium** | Professional enough for B2B. Accessible enough for consumers. |
| **Emotion** | **Calm confidence** | Inspire without pressure. Let clarity do the persuading. |
| **Energy** | **Measured & crisp** | Hero: punchy. Body sections: structured. CTA: decisive. |
| **Confidence** | **Strong & grounded** | Claims anchored to real specs and certifications. |

**STRUCTURAL RULES**

→  H1 (hero): ≤30 characters (2 rows max in mobile version). Outcome-led. Sentence case. No punctuation.

→  Subheadline: ≤70 characters (2 rows max in mobile version). Expands the H1 — answers "so what?" or "for whom?"

→  Supporting line: one proof point or differentiator. ≤30 characters (1 row max in mobile version).

→  Section headlines: ≤60 characters (2 rows max in mobile version). Verb or noun-led. Sentence case.

→  Body paragraphs: ≤140 characters (3 rows max in mobile version). Active voice. No passive constructions.

→  CTA: Title Case, verb-first, 2–4 words. Primary CTA above the fold.

→  Connect hero copy to at least one pillar (Capture / Extract / Utilize).

## **05 Product Description**

Precise, trustworthy, and complete. Specs and benefits in equal measure, written for buyers who want to understand before they decide.

**TONE DIMENSIONS**

| **Dimension** | **Setting** | **What this means** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Formality** | **Medium–High** | Structured and credible. Reads like a trusted advisor, not a salesperson. |
| **Emotion** | **Composed** | Warmth comes from clarity and confidence, not enthusiasm. |
| **Energy** | **Deliberate** | Unhurried. Each feature earns its own sentence. |
| **Confidence** | **Quietly authoritative** | State specs as facts. No hedging ("around," "approximately") unless technically accurate. |

**STRUCTURAL RULES**

→  Open with the product's primary job — what it does, for whom, in one sentence.

→  Feature structure: Feature name → what it does → why it matters. One feature per bullet.

→  Specs: always numeral + space + unit (e.g. 10 g, 256GB, 15 min). Never approximate unless certified.

→  Certifications listed individually and in full: SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, ISO 27701, GDPR, EN18031.

→  Adjectives: use sparingly. Prefer: intelligent, reliable, trusted, refined. Avoid: amazing, powerful, seamless.

→  Close with the product's position: category leadership or certification proof point.

**DO / DON'T — EXAMPLES**

| **✓  DO** | **✕  DON'T** |
| --- | --- |
| *Plaud NotePin S is a wearable AI note taker designed for all-day comfort and hands-free capturing.* | *Plaud NotePin S is an amazing, powerful, cutting-edge AI device that revolutionizes how you take notes!* |
| AI transcription in 112 languages with speaker labels and custom vocabulary. Generate multidimensional summaries with 10,000+ templates, mind maps, and workflow integrations. | *Super smart AI that amazingly understands everything you say and turns it into notes seamlessly and effortlessly.* |
| At 0.61 oz light, enjoy 20 hours of continuous recording, 40 days of standby, and 64GB of storage. | *Massive storage capacity! Tons of space for all your recordings so you never run out of room.* |
| Plaud adheres to the highest compliance standards including SOC 2 Type 2, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001/27701 and EN 18031. | *Very secure with lots of certifications. Your privacy is our top priority and we never misuse your data, ever!* |
| Choose the lanyard, wristband, clip, or magnetic pin to match your routine. Stay professional and keep your intelligence within reach all day. | *Revolutionary innovative clip design that seamlessly and effortlessly integrates into your professional lifestyle!* |

## **06 Video Scripts**

Video copy at Plaud covers formats like Unboxing and Quick Start Guide, serving a different moment in the viewer's journey. Both share the same voice: calm, composed, and free of performance. The tone and structure diverge based on purpose.

**TONE DIMENSIONS**

| **Dimension** | **Setting** | **What this means** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Formality** | **Low–Medium** | Conversational but composed. Relaxed enough to feel human, precise enough to build trust. |
| **Emotion** | **Warm & composed** | Unboxing: appreciative of craft and detail. Quick start: reassuring and steady. Neither is excitable. |
| **Energy** | **Unhurried** | Unboxing: slow at moments of reveal. Quick start: steady and methodical, never rushed. |
| **Confidence** | **Quietly assured** | Describe what is there. Give clear instructions. Let the product and the process do the work. |

**STRUCTURAL RULES**

→  — UNBOXING —

→  Script structure: Opening context (15 sec) → Box presentation (15 sec) → Each item reveal (10–15 sec each) → Hero product moment (20–30 sec) → Close (15 sec).

→  Describe what you see, feel, and observe, not what the viewer should feel.

→  Materials and finish: precise adjectives only (matte, brushed, magnetic). Not vague ones (premium, beautiful, amazing).

→  Spec mentions: woven naturally into description. "...and at 10 grams, it barely registers." Not recited as a list.

→  No countdown energy. No "Let's GO!" No artificial anticipation.

→  Transitions: slow and physical. "And underneath that..." / "Turning it over..." / "The last thing in the box..."

→  — QUICK START GUIDE —

→  Script structure: What it does (10 sec) → What's in the box (15 sec) → Setup steps (60–90 sec) → First use (30 sec) → Wrap (10 sec).

→  Each step: one action per sentence. Maximum 12 words per instruction.

→  Use second-person ("you", "your") throughout. No passive voice.

→  Transitions: "Next," "Now," "Once that's done," never "Subsequently" or "Following this."

→  Never end a step ambiguously. "You'll hear a chime" confirms success. "The app should open" does not.

→  If a technical term is necessary, define it in the same sentence.

**DO / DON'T — EXAMPLES**

| **✓  DO** | **✕  DON'T** |
| --- | --- |
| *[Unboxing] The box itself is minimal: matte black, no excess. That restraint carries through to everything inside.* | *[Unboxing] OH WOW guys, this packaging is absolutely INCREDIBLE! I am SO excited to see what's inside!* |
| *[Unboxing] This is the NotePin S. Ten grams. About the size of a thumbnail. It clips to a collar, a lapel, wherever you want it.* | *[Unboxing] Here it is: the REVOLUTIONARY NotePin S, the game-changer that's going to completely transform your life!* |
| *[Unboxing] The finish is matte. The edges are clean. It doesn't announce itself, which is exactly the point.* | *[Unboxing] The premium, ultra-luxurious design is stunning. You can really feel the innovative, cutting-edge craftsmanship.* |
| *[Quick start] Press and hold the button on the right side until the light turns blue. That means it's on.* | *[Quick start] To initiate the power-on sequence, depress the lateral activation button until the LED indicator displays blue illumination.* |
| *[Quick start] Tap Record. Say something. Anything. Then tap Stop.* | *[Quick start] Initiate a test recording by activating the record function and verbalising some content before terminating the capture session.* |
